## Component naming cross-check

The four BahamutBlitz blade components are encoded as:

- Bahamut Lock Chip
- Break Over Blade
- Blitz Metal Blade
- Knuckle Assist Blade

The official CX-16 page confirms the four-part system; the component names were cross-checked against current product documentation/catalog listings. These names are inventory facts, not performance statistics.

## Model boundary

Qualitative traits in `src/data.js` are weak modeling inputs derived from product descriptions and mechanical form. They are intentionally compressed and must not be interpreted as official Takara Tomy ratings or measured win probabilities.
