# Start here

1. Open `X-Tournament-Director-Evidence-First.html` or `index.html`.
2. The owned collection starts at zero.
3. Add sets by product code, browse the catalog, enter an unlisted set, add loose parts, or paste a list.
4. Autopilot runs when a legal 3-on-3 allocation becomes possible.
5. Open **Deck**. Review the legal deck, decision confidence, readiness gates, and the next decisive action.
6. A prior-only result is a **Hypothesis**. Record the recommended three-battle batches to move through Developing and Contender status.
7. Use **Match** for the official first-to-four score sheet and opponent-specific order guidance.
