Player guide

Four-step workflow

1

Add your collection

Mark owned Bey products or add individual loose parts. Quantities matter because two Beys cannot share one physical part during a test.

2

Build a legal deck

Use the manual editor or the optimizer. The app checks completeness, duplicate parts, and owned capacity.

3

Run guided battles

Assemble the exact two shown Beys, battle once, and save the result. The queue rotates legal pairings, including attack-movement matchups, while limiting immediate repeats.

4

Follow the command center

Readiness grows only when the deck is legal and evidence covers every Bey, multiple opponent roles, finish routes, and self-KO risk.
