How to use X Deck Lab

  1. 1
    Add your parts

    Open Parts. Browse official releases by date, including sets and part packs. Releases with no Beyblade performance parts are excluded.

  2. 2
    Build a deck

    Open Decks. Choose three Beys, or let the app suggest legal decks from your parts.

  3. 3
    Run the next test

    Open Test. Tap the first card. Build the shown opponent, battle once, and save the result.

  4. 4
    Check readiness

    Open Coach. It gives you one next mission, shows progress, and explains what needs work.

Self-KO means: your own Bey jumps or runs out mostly by itself. Just answer Yes or No—no special cause is needed.

For younger players: the main path is Parts → Decks → Test → Coach. The More screen contains advanced settings and backup tools; ask an adult for help there.

Quick words

Owned: You have the part. Deck: Your three Beys. Opponent: The Bey you test against. Finish: Spin, Over, Burst, or Xtreme.
